How it works

Fixing your neighborhood is easy.

CivicConnect bridges the gap between citizens and administration using a shared real-time backend.

1

Spot and Snap

See a pothole or broken streetlight? Open the app, take a picture, and hit submit. Your GPS location is attached automatically.

2

Gain Community Support

Your post appears in the public feed. Neighbors can upvote the issue, signaling higher priority to local administrators.

3

Track the Fix

Admins using the CivicConnect web dashboard review the hotspot map and dispatch teams. You get notified the moment the status changes to 'Resolved'.
